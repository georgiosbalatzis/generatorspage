About Us

Your Trusted Power Partner in Greece

Since 2005, Pourtsidis Generators has been providing high-quality generator solutions to businesses, construction sites, events, and homeowners throughout Greece. Based in Athens with service centers across major islands, we pride ourselves on our technical expertise and commitment to customer satisfaction.

Our team of certified engineers and technicians ensures that every generator we sell or rent meets the highest standards of performance and reliability, even in the most challenging environments.

  • 15+ years of experience in the Greek market
  • Nationwide service network
  • 24/7 technical support
  • Certified maintenance team
